Overview

This report was commissioned by the U.S. Committee on Foreign Relations in 1985 as a supplementary document to their earlier report titled “The Situation in the Philippines.” It was based on three weeks of field research conducted mainly in Negros Island and parts of Mindanao. Through interviews with government officials, military commanders, and local civilians, the document presents an assessment of the counterinsurgency efforts being carried out in the Philippines at that time.
