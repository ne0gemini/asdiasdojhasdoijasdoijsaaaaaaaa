About DataVault

A digital archive preserving historical records from the Martial Law era.

About the Project

The DataVault is a digital archive that consists of Social, Economic, Political and Cultural issues and records during the Martial Law period, under the presidency of former Philippine President Ferdinand Emmanuel Edralin Marcos Sr.

Our School

This project was created by students as part of a historical research activity. The website serves as both a learning project and a digital archive that highlights important events, news, and perspectives during the Martial Law era.
